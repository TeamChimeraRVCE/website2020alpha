ABOUT US

Started in the year 2006, Team Chimera is the first group in India to successfully transcend into the automotive sector of hybrid and electric technology. We are a multi-dimensional team comprising of students with back grounds in various engineering disciplines such as Electrical and Electronics, Mechanical, and Computer Science, apart from a dedicated team to look after the management and business development aspects of the team.

Team Chimera has, till date, undertaken various ventures, which include: E-volve Triple Powered Vehicle(TPV), Hybrid Auto Rickshaw, a plugin hybrid from the Electric Vehicle - REVA and the latest being building a Formula Hybrid race car and Formula Electric race car from scratch.

With the cost of gasoline skyrocketing to new heights and the predictions concerning its depletion within the next 20 years, electric and hybrid automotive technology provides a logical solution.

Our participation in recent events such as Formula Hybrid Competition, USA, Formula Electric Competition, Italy and Student Formula, Japan, Formula Green and Formula Bharat have been immensely successful and we continue to strive for progress and expansion in these environment friendly sectors.

OUR HISTORY

2006

Our Humble Beginnings

In 2006, a group of students from the Mechanical Department of RVCE came up with the idea of fuel efficient yet robust cars giving rise to TEAM CHIMERA.

2009

Hybrid Auto Rickshaw Battle

Our business plan was adjudged the best and the project overall finished second.

2011

Challenge Bibendum

The only Indian team to successfully participate in Challenge Bibendum organized by Michelin in Berlin.

2014

FSAE Hybrid - USA

First Asian team to participate. Stood 9th in the competition bringing back the Fan Favourite Award.

2015

FSAE Electric - Italy

First venture into pure Electric cars after spending years in making Hybrid cars which made use of gas along with electricity for propulsion.

2016

FSAE Electric - Japan

With improved car E-VOLVE v2 we bagged ROOKIE AWARD in the Electric Vehicle category

2017

FSAE Electric - Japan

We were placed 6th in business presentation in the electric category with our new car Chronos
